International Mobility

I have always been deeply interested in discovering new cultures. Thanks to the Internet, I have been able to connect with people from all around the world. However, the culture that truly captivated me is the German one.

I have made several trips to Germany and maintained a long-term exchange with a correspondent from Tübingen. These experiences helped me improve my language skills and develop a genuine appreciation for German values and lifestyle.

Therefore, I am considering completing my international mobility in Germany to strengthen both my technical and intercultural skills.
