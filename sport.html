Sport & Other Activities

Sports have always been important to me. They help me stay healthy, focused, and motivated. I think they also teach values that are useful for studying engineering.

I practice karate, football, and swimming. These sports taught me teamwork, precision, and concentration.

I am also passionate about electronics and aeronautics. I got my Aeronautical Initiation Certificate (BIA) in 2021. I like helping other students by giving maths and physics lessons.
